Origin Story

The Story of This Tree

Harvested from the banks of the Susquehanna River Valley, this Black Walnut stood for over 120 years before naturally succumbing to a storm in 2022. The "River" character in this slab comes from the deep, swirling grain patterns formed where the trunk navigated a split near the base.

"Every slab we dry carries the heritage of the Appalachian forest. WS-104 is particularly rare for its consistent thickness and lack of structural checking across such a wide span."
