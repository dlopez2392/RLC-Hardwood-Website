Wood Math Tips

  • 1

    The Basic Formula (T" x W" x L") / 144 = Board Feet. Ensure all dimensions are in inches for this calculation.

  • 2

    Wastage Factor Always add 10-15% to your final BF count to account for knots, sapwood, and milling losses.

  • 3

    Quarter Rules Remember that 4/4 is 1" thick, 8/4 is 2" thick, and so on. Rough-sawn is usually thicker than surfaced wood.
